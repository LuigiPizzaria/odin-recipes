Pizza

This is a veggie pizza but i just call it a pizza, find the ingredients below. this is a very easy recipe, very good for even beginners. You need an oven though.

  • dough
  • cheese (optional)
  • tomate sauce (optional)
  • veggies (optional)
  1. Roll out the dough
  2. Putt all the stuff you like on it
  3. Put that thing in the oven
  4. take a deep breath
  5. will soon be finished, then take out of the oven
  6. eat

If you don't include the optional ingredients, you will just have baked bread.
